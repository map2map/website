FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Map2Map™?

Map2Map™ is a professional Google Maps management service that helps businesses optimize their online presence. We handle everything from creating new listings to maintaining and updating existing ones, ensuring your business is accurately represented on Google Maps.

Why should I choose Map2Map™?

We offer expert management of your Google Maps listing, saving you time and ensuring accuracy. Our team handles all updates, optimizations, and maintenance, allowing you to focus on running your business while we take care of your online presence.

How often can I update my profile?

We offer flexible update schedules based on your chosen plan. From monthly reviews to weekly updates, we ensure your business information stays current and accurate. Enterprise clients can request updates as needed.

What information can you update?

We can update all aspects of your Google Maps listing, including business hours, contact information, photos, descriptions, services, and more. We also handle review responses and maintain your business's online reputation.

How long does it take to see results?

Initial setup and optimization typically takes 1-2 weeks. Ongoing updates are processed within 24-48 hours. The impact on your visibility can vary, but most clients see improvements within the first month.

Do you offer support for multiple locations?

Yes! Our Enterprise plan is specifically designed for businesses with multiple locations. We can manage all your locations under one account, ensuring consistent branding and information across all listings.
